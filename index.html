Canada Work Visa Application Details & Fees (2026),

Work Permit File process & Fee: CAD 210 Per person. and

Biometrics Fee: CAD 85 + CAD 100 per person (or CAD 180 Per Family),

Open Work Permit Holder Fee: An additional CAD 100 for IEC or similar programs.

Processing Time: Varies heavily, typically 30–90 days, but some applications (like IEC) may process in as little as 2 weeks.

Requirements:

A valid job offer from a Canadian employer. A Labour Market Impact Assessment (LMIA) from the employer (unless exempt). Proof of funds (approximately CAD 2,500). Valid passport, medical exam, and police clearance certificates.

Permanent Residence Pathways (2026),

Standard Application: CAD 1,525 (including right of permanent residence fee). Special Pathways: Fees for caregivers and specific pilots are around CAD 1,210. Note: Fees and processing times are subject to change by Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC).
